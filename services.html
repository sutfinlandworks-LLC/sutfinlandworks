Our Services

Professional Land Maintenance and Property Improvement Services Throughout Mid-Michigan

Driveway Grading

We restore gravel and dirt driveways using professional grading equipment to eliminate ruts, potholes, washboarding, and uneven surfaces.

Benefits of Driveway Grading

  • Reduces potholes and washboarding
  • Improves drainage and minimizes standing water
  • Extends the life of your driveway
  • Creates a smoother driving surface
  • Improves curb appeal and property value

Rototilling

Our 60-inch rotary tiller prepares gardens, food plots, landscaping areas, and new lawn sites by breaking up compacted soil and incorporating organic matter.

Benefits of Rototilling

  • Loosens compacted soil for improved root growth
  • Creates an ideal seedbed for planting
  • Mixes compost and nutrients into the soil
  • Helps control weeds and unwanted vegetation
  • Improves drainage and soil aeration

Flail Mowing

Our flail mower is ideal for maintaining overgrown fields, trails, fence lines, ditches, vacant lots, and difficult terrain. It can safely process grass, brush, and woody vegetation up to approximately 1.5 inches in diameter.

Benefits of Flail Mowing

  • Controls heavy vegetation and brush growth
  • Produces a cleaner finish than many traditional brush cutters
  • Improves property appearance and accessibility
  • Reduces habitat for pests and unwanted wildlife
  • Excellent for trails, field edges, banks, and ditches
  • Helps maintain fence lines and property boundaries
